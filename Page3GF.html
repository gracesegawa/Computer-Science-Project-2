Gasoline Facts

Some interesteing facts about the usage of gasoline.

What do consumers use gasoline in?

  • Vehicles for transportation: Cars, SUV, light trucks, and motorcycles
  • Recreational vehicles: Motorhomes, boats, off road vehicles
  • Small planes
  • Work: Construction equipment and tools, farming, forestry and landscaping
  • Power: Fuel for portable and emergency power supply

How much gasoline do consumers use?

  • According to the US Department of Energy Transportation accounted for 69% of U.S. petroleum use in 2018 and was the most for any sector.
  • Light-duty vehicles (cars, sport utility vehicles, and small trucks) account for about 90% of all gasoline consumption in the United States.
