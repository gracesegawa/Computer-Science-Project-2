Enviromental Effects of Gasoline

This page gives more information on negative impacts that gasoline has on our environment and health.

Harmful Chemicals in Gasoline

Hydrocarbons
  • Ingesting any amount of hydrocarbons found in gasoline can be fatal.
  • When even small quantities of hydrocarbons enter the bloodstream, it can reduce functioning of the central nervous system and cause organ damage.

Carbon monoxide
  • Colorless, odorless gas that is deadly when people inhale it.
  • Burning gasoline releases carbon monoxide which is toxic.
  • Extended exposure cause serious health complications.
